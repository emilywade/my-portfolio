Management Information (MI) Rebuild
This was by far my most pertinent project. I was the project lead in charge of rebuilding an outdated SQL MI process in SAS Enterprise Guide. I simplified existing data pulls and manipulations which reduced the overall MI refresh time by 91%, so that the process spanned one morning over the original 3 days. Team-wide transparency was a large issue before my arrival, so I ensured everything was appropriately documented throughout the project. Once the foundations were in place and proper testing had taken place, I automated all data extrapolations and exports to Excel to further reduce on human error, costs and time. Alongside the programming, I developed a technical Excel pack to aid the monthly analysis of outputs, including forecasting and error detection tools.

Analytical Inefficiencies

Key deliverables included binary compression on large datasets, timestamps to reduce folder capacity and implementation of SAS scheduler and nightqueue. We comitted to regualar meetings and goal-setting sessions to ensure deadlines were met and deliverables were consisistent and visible.
